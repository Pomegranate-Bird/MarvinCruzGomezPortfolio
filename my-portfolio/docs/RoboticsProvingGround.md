## Introduction
Contributed to the Robotics Proving Grounds, an educational robotics projects. Contributed to the project through the creation of the Lidar, Binary Occupancy Grid Mapping, and Probailistic Occupancy Grip Mapping teaching modules.
<br>

![Robotics Proving Grounds](images/RoboticsProvingGround/lidar.png)
![Robotics Proving Grounds](images/RoboticsProvingGround/binaryOgm.png)

<br>
<br>

Through the development of a Binary OGM and Probabilistic OGM mapping algorithm, I explained what the role of mapping algorithms serve within the world of autonomous robotics but also how they work. Additionally contributed to the testing and integration of the OGM algorithms in Pybullet, a physics simulator. Additionally created and explained a control algorithm, the Stanely Controller, it's uses, how it works and implemented it within Pybullet.
